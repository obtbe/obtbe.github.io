    <div class="about-bio">
      <p>Growing up, I loved numbers and the outdoors. My grandad, a horse trainer in Bamako, Mali, shaped that passion. We’d ride at his stable, where the scent of hay filled the air. He showed me how a small shift in balance could steer a horse, blending math with nature’s rhythm. Those lessons guide me still.</p>
      <p>Today, I analyze data to bring clarity to tough problems. I started Quantidum, a data analytics firm, to help businesses make smart choices using tools like Python and SQL. Each project teaches me something new. I also founded MansaPi, a non-profit in Mali, to get kids excited about STEM. We run workshops where students code simple apps or build solar-powered lights, hoping to inspire future problem-solvers.</p>
      <p>My blog shares ideas on data and tech. I hope it sparks curiosity in you.</p>
    </div>
