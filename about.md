    <div class="bio-text">
      <p>I grew up chasing numbers and nature in Bamako, Mali. My grandad, a horse trainer, showed me both. At his stable, where dust and hay hung in the air, he taught me to ride. He’d point out how a slight tilt could steer a horse, tying math to the animal’s stride. Those moments still shape me.</p>
      <p>Now, I turn data into answers. I founded Quantidum, a data analytics firm, to help businesses solve problems with tools like Python and SQL. Every project pushes me to learn. I also started MansaPi, a non-profit in Mali to get kids into STEM. We teach them to code simple games or build solar lamps, hoping to light up their curiosity.</p>
      <p>This blog is my space to share data and tech ideas. I hope it inspires you.</p>
      <blockquote class="quote">
        “Yesterday I was clever, so I wanted to change the world. Today I am wise, so I am changing myself.” — Rumi
